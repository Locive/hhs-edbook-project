ABOUT US

Our mission

Our mission is to make life easier for students and teachers by offering an app that organizes what supplies and resources are located at their schools. This allows students and teachers to see which schools need certain resources and which schools may have those supplies. Overall, we hope our app will allow for schools to work together and create a sharing community, which will in turn benefit the students and help them succeed.
