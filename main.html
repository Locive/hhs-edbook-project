About us

Edbook is a service that will allow schools to get more ideas on getting school supplies and to give away school supplies to other schools. Let's face the fact that all schools are missing some supplies that are necessary for kids to work. Edbook will make it easier for schools to get supplies that they need without spending money on new supplies by trading with other schools on supplies you need.
